CEO's Message

Dear Parents,

“ Education is not the learning of facts, but the training of the mind to think. ” -Albert Einstein

I feel honoured and privileged to be part of an educational institution where every stakeholder is a learner and every day is an opportunity to learn and discover. We look at ourselves as a community of learners, where everyone learns including our teachers, parents & staff. Our staff fruitfully employs two diverse strategies that are of Love and Logic to foster a positive learning environment for all our students. Love and Logic may seem like two contrasting forces. While love helps nurture trusting relationships, where students feel respected, appreciated and loved by the teachers, logic helps develop in students personal responsibility, self control, good decision making skills self confidence, and character building with high moral values.

I appreciate the efforts put in by our wonderful team of Teaching and non teaching staff, and the unstinting support of the Management for making this a truly successful year. It was a year of achievements and victories, of facing new challenges and reaching greater heights. Our curriculum and practices are continually gauged to corroborate the most updated and best practices which address the future needs of our students. It is our responsibility to pass on the legacy of education to our future generations where it sets children to grow personally, professionally and socially. It can awaken joy, curiosity a deep desire to solve problems and to be self-responsible individuals.

Balyam – a school with an unique idea for the development of new India (Sampoorna Bharath) focusing on the holistic development of the child, with the great support of the parent. We have chain of well flourished schools in Telangana especially in Northern Telangana and are planning to flourish all over Telangana and nearby states. I am confident over your child development as I believe in the unique curriculum as I said where teacher and parents are learners with the child. I wish you all good luck and let’s work harder to achieve bigger and better milestones in the coming years. Remember, success is not final. Success is not resting on your laurels, but finding new laurels to rest upon. At last I end with a great thought of my role model “Learning gives creativity, creativity leads to thinking, thinking provides knowledge, and knowledge makes you great.” ~ Dr.APJ. Abdul Kalam

Namaskaram.
Swaraj Gopalapuram
Founder & CEO
